FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is there an age limit?

Yes, all attendees must be 21+ with a valid ID. This is strictly enforced at the door.

How long is the show?

All of our shows run between 75-90 minutes. There is no intermission.

Can I bring a group?

Absolutely! Groups are welcome. Since seating is first come, first served, we recommend arriving early to ensure your group can sit together.

Is there an item minimum?

There is a 2-item minimum and a bartender will be available for patrons to order food & drinks.

Can I buy tickets at the door?

We recommend purchasing tickets in advance as many shows sell out. If tickets are available, they may be purchased at the door for an additional fee.

If it's sold out, can I still attend?

Yes! We will be offering a small amount of tickets available at the door for $20, however we cannot guarantee that your party will be seated together.

What is the refund policy?

All sales are final. No refunds or exchanges. If you cannot attend, you may be able to receive a refund by contacting us at hausofcomedy@gmail.com.

Where can I park?

Street parking is available in the area, and there are several paid parking garages nearby. We recommend arriving with extra time to find parking.
